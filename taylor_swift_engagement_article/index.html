Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce Announce Engagement After Two Years of Dating

The pop superstar and NFL star shared their engagement news on Instagram with photos from a romantic garden proposal, breaking platform records with millions of likes.

By Entertainment Tonight August 26, 2025 2.3M views 11 hours ago
Taylor Swift & Travis Kelce Engagement Announcement

In a moment that sent shockwaves across social media and broke Instagram records, pop superstar Taylor Swift and Kansas City Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce announced their engagement on Tuesday afternoon with a heartwarming Instagram post that garnered over 14 million likes in just one hour.

The couple shared a carousel of five intimate photos from their romantic proposal, which took place in a beautiful garden setting in Lee's Summit, Missouri, nearly two weeks ago. The images capture Kelce on one knee surrounded by flowers, with Swift wearing a striped summer dress, visibly emotional as she accepts his proposal.

"Your English teacher and your gym teacher are getting married," the couple wrote in their joint Instagram caption, referencing Swift's academic background and Kelce's athletic career.

A Love Story Two Years in the Making

The engagement marks the culmination of a whirlwind romance that began in 2023 when Kelce made a bold move on his "New Heights" podcast, admitting he had tried to give Swift his phone number on a friendship bracelet during her Eras Tour stop in Kansas City.

"This all started when Travis very adorably put me on blast on his podcast, which I thought was metal as hell," Swift told Time Magazine in December 2023. "We started hanging out right after that. So we actually had a significant amount of time that no one knew, which I'm grateful for, because we got to get to know each other."

The Proposal Details

According to Travis's father, Ed Kelce, who spoke to News 5 Cleveland, the proposal took place during what was supposed to be a casual evening. Travis suggested getting a glass of wine before dinner, setting the perfect romantic atmosphere for the life-changing moment.

The engagement ring, designed by Travis and Kindred Lubeck of Artifex Fine Jewelry in New York City, features a stunning old-mine brilliant cut diamond estimated between 10-12 carats, set in yellow gold. The vintage-style ring perfectly reflects Swift's classic aesthetic preferences.

Record-Breaking Announcement

The Instagram post made platform history, becoming one of the fastest posts to reach 1 million reposts in less than six hours. Meta confirmed that Instagram briefly experienced issues due to the overwhelming response, with a spokesperson joking, "Turns out Instagram needed to process this news, just like everyone else."

Looking Ahead

The engagement comes at an exciting time for both stars. Swift recently announced her 12th studio album, "The Life of a Showgirl," during an appearance on Kelce's podcast, while Travis prepares for what could be his final NFL season as the Chiefs aim for their third consecutive Super Bowl title.

As fans around the world celebrate this modern-day fairytale, one thing is certain: Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have proven that sometimes the most beautiful love stories happen when you least expect them.
